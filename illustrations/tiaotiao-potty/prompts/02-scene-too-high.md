# Prompt: 马桶好高呀

## STYLE
Flat illustration, children's book style, soft pastel colors, rounded shapes, expressive characters, minimalist background, cute cartoon style.

## SUBJECT
Tiaotiao (2-year-old Chinese boy, medium hair, round face) standing on his tiptoes, struggling to climb onto a large adult toilet. He looks determined but the toilet is clearly too big for him. Dad (round steamed bun character) is holding a small step ladder steady. Mom (cute hedgehog) watches with an encouraging smile, hands clasped together.

## COMPOSITION
Center: Large white toilet, Tiaotiao trying to climb
Left: Dad bun holding ladder
Right: Mom hedgehog cheering
Foreground: Step ladder
Background: Bathroom wall with simple tiles, towel rack.

## COLORS
Soft white, light blue, warm beige, gentle peach, pastel yellow.

## MOOD
Supportive, slightly challenging but warm, parents helping child overcome obstacle.

## DETAILS
- Tiaotiao's arms reaching up, one foot on ladder
- Dad bun has small arms holding ladder firmly
- Mom hedgehog has kind eyes watching
- Exaggerate size difference between toilet and child for cute effect
- Simple geometric shapes for bathroom elements
